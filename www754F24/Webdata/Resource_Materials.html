Transportation

  • TL, LTL, and PX PPI, select Series ID=PCU484121484121 (TL), Series ID=PCU484122484122 (LTL), and Series ID=PCU492110492110 (PX: Couriers and express delivery services, not seasonally adjusted) for current monthly and annual PPI values
  • FedEx LTL and PX rates, provide one-time (spot) LTL and PX rate quotes
  • Coyote Logistics (a UPS Company) rate quotes for TL and LTL
  • Glossary of Shipping Terms from U.S. Department of Transportation
  • National Highway Planning Network (NHPN), the Federal Highway Administration's road network database

Geocoding and Mapping

  • FIPS Codes: 
    • State FIPS Codes: two-digit FIPS State Numeric Codes and the two-letter FIPS State Alpha Code for each state
    • County FIPS Codes: five-digit FIPS County Numeric Codes for each county in each state, where three county digits are appended to two state digits.
  • Coastline Extractor, extract coastline segments bounded by a specified latitude/longitude range
  • How far is it?, calculates great circle distances (same as DISTS using 'mi')
  • Aviation Formulary, has information on great circle distances
  • GIS FAQ Question --Q5.1--: What is the best way to calculate the great circle distance (which deliberately ignores elevation differences) between 2 points? (originally at http://www.census.gov/cgi-bin/geo/gisfaq?Q5.1)

TSP and VRP
